# Beginner → Endgame Roadmap

A progression path from broke to a self-sustaining money empire. Tailor to the player's current cash/rank.

## Phase 0 — Broke (under ~$500k)

- **Don't buy a flashy car yet.** Build income first.
- Do the **First Dose** missions → unlock the **free Acid Lab** (install it; free with GTA+, else $750k — save up).
- Run the **Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid** (free, ~$500k + $250k first-time) — your first big lump sum, fully solo.
- Do **daily collectibles** (Hidden Caches $100k/day, LS Tags $75k/day) and **Daily Objectives** for the streak.
- Spin the **Lucky Wheel** daily (need a Penthouse later, else use the casino) for a chance at a free podium car.
- Goal: reach ~$1M+ to start buying income property.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (~$1M–3M)

- Buy the **Acid Lab equipment upgrade** ($250k) — now ~$120k/hr passive, sell from anywhere.
- Buy a **Nightclub** ($1.08M–1.7M) near your other businesses; assign technicians; keep popularity up. Your AFK backbone.
- Buy an **MC Clubhouse + Cocaine Lockup** (feeds the nightclub + sells well).
- Keep grinding Cluckin' Bell + dailies; sell Acid Lab/Cocaine on bonus weeks.
- Goal: save for the Kosatka.

## Phase 2 — The income engine (~$2.2M for the Kosatka)

- Buy the **Kosatka** → unlock **Cayo Perico**. This is the turning point — ~$1.1M+ per ~1 hr run, solo.
- Run Cayo on repeat; during its cooldown, sell businesses, run dailies, and (next) Agency contracts.
- Buy the **Sparrow** (Kosatka helicopter) for fast prep skips.
- Goal: Cayo loop funds everything else.

## Phase 3 — Active income & heist access (~$2M each)

- Buy the **Agency** → Dr. Dre ($1M) + payphone/security loop (~$450k/hr) — best Cayo-cooldown filler.
- Buy an **Arcade** → unlock the **Diamond Casino Heist** (do the **Signal Jammers** first to unlock Avi the free hacker). Find a partner for diamonds weeks.
- Buy a **Bunker** for big passive sells + a nightclub feeder.

## Phase 4 — Rounding out (~$2M each)

- **Salvage Yard** (~$1M/week from 3 robberies), **Auto Shop** (~$300k/hr robbery contracts).
- **Money Fronts** (Car Wash + Smoke on the Water) only if you grind Counterfeit/Weed (+35% each).
- **Bail Office** for variety + the daily Most Wanted ($120k).

## Phase 5 — Endgame / optimization

- Full passive stack feeding the nightclub (Coke/Meth/Cash techs); sell only on 2x weeks.
- **(Optional) Mansion** ($11.5M+) for the daily +66% production boost across businesses + remote management — buy this last, it amplifies an existing empire.
- Run Cayo + Casino (with a partner on alternating cooldowns); keep every business producing; clear dailies.

## Money mistakes beginners make

- Buying expensive cars/cosmetics before income property.
- Buying Document Forgery / base Weed expecting good returns (low ROI).
- Selling in hostile public lobbies and losing the batch (sell invite-only).
- Ignoring the **weekly 2x event** and selling at base rates.
- Letting businesses sit empty (no supplies) or full (unsold) — both waste money.
- Forgetting **free** income: dailies, objectives streak, Lucky Wheel, Stash Houses.
