## Nightclub — the passive backbone (set & forget)

- **Two income streams:** (1) the **popularity safe** (up to $50k/in-game day, caps $250k); (2) the **warehouse**, where up to 5 **technicians** passively accrue goods from your *other owned businesses* (those businesses only need to be owned + set up — no resupply needed to feed the club).
- **Best technician assignments by value:** South American Imports (Coke, ~$13.5k/hr) > Pharmaceutical Research (Meth, ~$11.5k/hr) > Cash Creation (~$9.5k/hr) > Cargo & Shipments (Hangar, ~$8.6k/hr) > Sporting Goods (Bunker, ~$7.5k/hr). Skip Organic Produce (Weed) and Printing & Copying (Forgery) unless that's all you own.
- **Full 360-unit warehouse ≈ $1.93M (≈ $1.74M after Tony's 10%).** Sell in one vehicle.
- **Popularity** decays; run a promo/management interaction to top it back to 100% so the safe keeps filling. Buy the **equipment upgrade + extra storage floors**.
- **Empty the safe before it caps** (~$250k) so it keeps generating.

## Acid Lab — best cheap solo business

- Unlock the lab **free** via the 6 **First Dose** missions, then $750k to install (free with GTA+), or buy the **MTL Brickade 6×6 ($1.45M)**. Get the **$250k equipment upgrade** (requires 10 Fooligan jobs to unlock).
- Resupply $60k via the phone (Mutt); **sell from anywhere** in one short single-vehicle delivery — no warehouse trips.
- Has its own **once-per-day Chemical production boost** — stack it with the **Mansion** boost for max throughput.
- Full 160 units = ~$335k upgraded; ~$120k/hr passive. The best solo passive business.

## Bunker (Gunrunning)

- Set to **Manufacturing**, not Research (Research only unlocks MkII weapon upgrades).
- Full upgraded ($1.155M equipment + $598k staff) makes ~$1.05M in ~12 h sold to LS.
- **Steal supplies** for free if you have time, else buy ($75k). **Sell before stock exceeds ~$175k** or solo sell missions spawn multiple/aircraft vehicles with tight timers.
- Feeds the Nightclub "Sporting Goods" tech.

## MC businesses (Cocaine > Meth > Counterfeit > Weed >> Document Forgery)

- Requires an MC **Clubhouse** ($200k–495k) to register. Clubhouse income itself is negligible.
- **Cocaine Lockup is the only must-buy** MC business. Meth and Counterfeit are good, **especially with Money Fronts boosts** (Car Wash +35% Counterfeit, Smoke on the Water +35% Weed). **Skip Document Forgery and base Weed** for active sells.
- All sell solo in one vehicle, or feed them to the Nightclub via technicians and never sell manually. Keep them stocked so they don't get raided.
- **Street Dealers** (daily) and **Stash Houses** (daily free resupply) pair perfectly with MC/Acid — see `references/collectibles-and-daily.md`.

## Money Fronts (laundering + permanent boosts)

- **Hands On Car Wash ($1M)** = hub + **+35% Counterfeit Cash** sales + ~$30k/in-game-day safe.
- **Smoke on the Water ($850k)** = **+35% Weed Farm** sales.
- **Higgins Helitours ($900k)** = **+10% Hangar cargo** value.
- Each has 4 laundering missions (~$35k, ~10 min, no cooldown) but the **permanent sale boosts are the reason to buy** — only worth it if you grind the boosted businesses. Laundering raises "Heat" (+10%/mission, 100% = raid); reset with one legit job.

## CEO Cargo, Hangar, Arcade

- **Vehicle Cargo:** top-range-only export (~$100k/car) + CEO work to fill cooldowns ≈ $430k/hr active; fully solo.
- **Special Cargo:** large warehouse 111 crates ≈ $2.2M but solo big-sells are painful; sell in batches or feed the nightclub.
- **Hangar:** big totals but tedious sells; best on 2x weeks; +10% from Higgins Helitours.
- **Arcade:** ~$5k/in-game day passive (cap $100k) — you buy it for **Diamond Casino Heist access**, not the income.

## The Mansion production boost (passive multiplier)

The **Mansion** (Prix Luxury Real Estate, Dec 2025; $11.5M–12.8M base, ~$17.5M maxed) makes **no income of its own**, but its **AI Assistant grants a once-daily, 24-hour, ~66% production-speed boost** to ONE eligible business (Acid Lab, Bunker, Cocaine, Meth, Weed, Counterfeit, Document Forgery — **not** the Nightclub warehouse). You must **rotate** which business you boost each day (can't boost the same one two days running). The **Master Control Terminal** also lets you manage all businesses remotely. Point the boost at whatever you'll actively sell that day (Acid Lab for solo, or whatever's on the weekly 2x). Full detail: `references/properties-catalog.md`.

## When to sell (priority order)

1. The business has a **2x/3x bonus this week** → sell now even if not full (MCP `gta-weekly-bonuses`).
2. Stock is **at/near your solo vehicle limit** → sell before it forces a multi-vehicle timed mission (esp. Bunker, Special Cargo, Hangar).
3. **Nightclub safe near cap (~$250k)** → empty it.
4. You're about to **log off** → sell everything; nothing should sit idle overnight.
5. **High-demand bonus** (public lobby, +2.5%/rival to +50%) only if you can survive the lobby; otherwise sell invite-only for guaranteed delivery. A guaranteed $1.05M beats a blown-up $1.575M.
