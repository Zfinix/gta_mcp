# Collectibles & Daily/Weekly Income

The supplemental income layer. Doing the daily resets is ~$300k+/day for ~30–40 min of low-skill work — great while businesses produce. Daily items reset **06:00 UTC**; weekly items reset **Thursday 07:00 UTC**. Use the MCP `gta-daily-collectibles` (today's positions) and `gta-collectible-locations` (all spawn points) tools.

## Daily objectives (3/day)

- All 3 = **$30,000 (+5,000 RP)**.
- **7-day streak = +$150,000**, **28-day streak = +$750,000**.
- Essentially free money — never break the streak. ~$210k/week keeping the 7-day streak.

## Casino dailies

- **Lucky Wheel:** 1 free daily spin (own a Penthouse). 1/20 chance at the **podium vehicle** (~$900k–1.6M). GTA+ gets a 2nd spin. The car is the real prize — basically a free ~$1M car per week if you win.
- **Casino work missions (Agatha Baker):** $5k + 5k chips each, fast; 50 missions = $180k bonus. Mainly for chips.

## Recurring / rotating income

- **Street Dealers:** each day one featured drug (Weed/Coke/Meth/Acid) bought at a premium — dump your MC/Acid product for bonus value with no delivery mission. Locations rotate daily.
- **Stash Houses:** 1/day. **$30,000** if you own no MC business, or a **free full resupply (~$75k value)** for one MC drug business. Weekly: raid 3 → +$100,000.
- **Gun Van:** daily rotating discount shop (weapons/armor/MkII ammo, 30–40% off, more with GTA+). A money-*saver*, not earner — check daily.
- **Madrazo Dispatch:** 6 assassination missions, ~$23k each; only worth it on a 2x bonus week.
- **LS Car Meet:** weekly **Prize Ride** = free ~$1M car for the weekly challenge (often "top 3 in Street Race series 3 days running"). Sprint/Pursuit/Street races pay GTA$ + RP + LS Rep (often 2x on event weeks).

## Weekly event (Thursday 07:00 UTC) — the biggest multiplier

- Rockstar rotates **2x/3x (sometimes 4x) GTA$ & RP** on specific businesses/heists/modes, plus **discounts** and a new **podium vehicle**.
- **Time your big sells and heists to whatever is bonused this week** — it can double your effective $/hr. Always check the weekly (MCP `gta-weekly-bonuses`) before grinding.

## Suggested daily order (fast)

1. Lucky Wheel spin → 2. Empty all business safes → 3. Stash House (free resupply) → 4. G's Cache + Shipwreck (fast singles) → 5. Hidden Caches ($100k) → 6. LS Tags ($75k) → 7. Junk Energy skydives → 8. Bail Office Most Wanted → 9. Salvage robbery (if available) → 10. Daily Objectives. Businesses produce the entire time.
