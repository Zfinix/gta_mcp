# GTA Online Glossary & Acronyms

Quick reference for terms used across this skill and by the community.

## Money & business terms

- **$/hr** — GTA$ earned per real-world hour; the key efficiency metric.
- **Passive income** — money that accrues without active play (nightclub safe, business production, property safes).
- **Active income** — money from doing missions/heists (Cayo, contracts).
- **High-demand bonus** — +2.5% per rival player (max +50%) when selling in a populated public lobby.
- **2x / 3x / 4x week** — the weekly Rockstar event multiplier on specific activities (resets Thursday 07:00 UTC).
- **Podium vehicle** — the Diamond Casino prize car from the Lucky Wheel, changes weekly.
- **Tunables** — the daily/weekly values Rockstar pushes to the game (Cayo target, daily collectible spawns, podium car, discounts). gtaweb.eu decodes these.
- **Supplies / stock / product** — supplies are consumed to produce stock/product, which you sell.
- **Sell mission / vehicle limit** — solo can only deliver so much per trip; overfilled businesses spawn multiple vehicles with tight timers.
- **Heat (Money Fronts)** — laundering meter; 100% triggers a raid; reset with a legit job.
- **Production boost** — the Mansion AI Assistant's once-daily 24h ~66% speed boost (rotated across businesses).

## Properties & businesses (acronyms)

- **MC** — Motorcycle Club (clubhouse + the 5 drug/forgery businesses).
- **CEO / VIP / SecuroServ** — the executive role from an Office; runs Special & Vehicle Cargo and VIP work.
- **NC** — Nightclub.
- **Kosatka** — the submarine that hosts the Cayo Perico Heist.
- **Brickade / Acid Lab** — the mobile MTL Brickade 2.0 lab.
- **Agency** — The Contract property (Dr. Dre, security contracts, payphone hits).
- **Salvage Yard / Chop Shop** — weekly vehicle robberies.
- **Bail Office** — Bottom Dollar Bounties bounty-hunting property.
- **Money Fronts** — Car Wash / Smoke on the Water / Higgins Helitours (laundering + sale boosts).
- **Mansion** — Prix Luxury Real Estate (Tongva Estate / Vinewood Residence / Richman Villa).

## Heists & activities

- **Cayo** — Cayo Perico Heist (best solo earner).
- **Casino Heist / DCH** — Diamond Casino Heist (needs 2 players).
- **Cluckin' Bell / CBFR** — Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid (free, solo).
- **Setup / prep** — preparation missions required before a heist finale.
- **Finale** — the payout mission of a heist.
- **Scope / scope out** — the Cayo recon mission that reveals loot/entry options.
- **Elite Challenge** — bonus payout for completing a heist under strict conditions.
- **Daily Objectives** — 3 randomized daily tasks; streak bonuses at 7 and 28 days.

## Combat & lobby

- **Mk II** — upgraded weapons (via Bunker research) that accept special ammo.
- **Explosive rounds** — Heavy Sniper Mk II ammo that one-shots most targets/vehicles.
- **Oppressor Mk II / MkII** — the flying motorcycle; common griefer/utility vehicle.
- **Passive Mode** — Interaction Menu toggle making you immune to player bullets (not explosives).
- **Off the Radar / Ghost Org** — abilities that hide you from other players' radar.
- **Griefer** — a player who attacks others for fun; avoid by selling in invite-only sessions.
- **Invite-only / solo public session** — private-ish lobbies safe for selling and setups.

## Time / resets

- **Daily reset** — 06:00 UTC (daily collectibles, Stash Houses, Most Wanted, daily objectives).
- **Weekly reset** — Thursday 07:00 UTC (weekly event, podium car, prize ride, Salvage robberies).
- **In-game day** — 48 real-world minutes (used for safe/passive income rates).
- **Cooldown** — the wait before you can repeat an activity (Cayo target rotation, Cluckin' Bell 60 min, etc.).
