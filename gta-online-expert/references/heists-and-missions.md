# Heists & Missions — Setups, Skips, What to Grind

## Cayo Perico — solo stealth walkthrough

**Goal:** infiltrate solo, stealth, grab secondary loot + primary target, exfil. ~$1.1M+ per run.

**Prep order:**
1. **Buy the Kosatka** (~$2.2M one-time) → start the heist from its planning screen (~$100k setup).
2. **Scope out the island** (the recon mission): mark guard clothing, a compound entry point, the grappling/cutting equipment, and the power station/control tower. Take photos of everything; this unlocks options. Scoping persists, so you only fully scope once.
3. **Gather equipment prep jobs:** typically *Cutting Torch / Grappling Equipment*, *Plasma Cutter or Bolt Cutters* (for secondary loot doors), and the *Approach* (drainage tunnel is the classic stealth entry). Optionally grab the *Sewer/Drainage* entry and disable *Air Defenses* if you want fewer alarms.
4. **Stealth route (drainage tunnel approach):**
   - Enter via the drainage tunnel (south of the island) to bypass most guards.
   - Disable the power/EMP if you set it up; use the guard outfit to blend.
   - Grab **secondary loot** first (cocaine/gold/weed/cash/paintings) — prioritize **gold** for $/bag, then cocaine. Crack safes in the office for extra cash + the primary in the basement vault.
   - Get the **primary target** from El Rubio's office/vault.
   - **Exfil** via the marked escape point (grappling spot, boat, or the main gate if loud) and swim/boat to the Kosatka.

**Tips:**
- Take a **suppressed weapon**, save your loadout. Headshot or avoid guards.
- The **Panther Statue** (~$1.9M) rotates in occasionally and is the best primary — run it when available.
- **Cooldown:** the finale has a replay cooldown; the same primary target is restricted for a longer window. Fill the cooldown with Agency contracts and business sells.

## Diamond Casino Heist

- **Requires** an Arcade. Setup fee ~$25k.
- **Pick your approach** based on prep tolerance:
  - **Big Con (disguise):** easiest reliable solo/duo — walk in, grab vault, walk out.
  - **Silent & Sneaky:** stealth, highest skill ceiling, avoids the heavy combat.
  - **Aggressive:** loud, fast, but you take heavy fire on exit; best with a strong crew.
- **Loot:** Diamonds (best, when available) > Gold > Artwork > Cash. The vault contents rotate.
- **Cut:** you keep ~ (100% − crew% − 10% fence). With a 2-player split you each net ~$700k–900k on diamonds.
- Can't repeat the same approach back-to-back — alternate.

## Agency — The Contract (Dr. Dre)

- Buy an **Agency**. Do the **VIP contract** storyline (find Dre's phone) — pays ~$1M finale, repeatable, plus ~$1.6M across the questline first time.
- **Security contracts** are the bread-and-butter filler: pick them from the agency computer, $31k–70k each, back-to-back, no sell vehicle, no cooldown.
- After 3 contracts, **payphone hits** unlock: ~$85k each if you complete the optional kill method.

## Salvage Yard robberies

- Three robbery types — 3 available per week. Each nets ~$260k–405k + ~$50k bonus. Plus a passive wall safe.

## Auto Shop contracts

- Service customer cars (small passive) and run **robbery contracts** ($153k–405k; Union Depository ~$300–375k is the best). Staff take ~10%.

## Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid (free, solo)

- **No property or buy-in.** Pick it up as a contact mission. Do the short setup steps (recon the factory, disable security, etc.), then the finale.
- **Finale ~$500k leader** + **$250k first-time bonus** + grab cash from registers/safes inside during the finale. ~30–40 min solo. **60-min cooldown** between runs.
- The best **free** earner for new/mid players — run it on cooldown early game while you save for the Kosatka.

## Classic heists & Doomsday (do once, then skip for money)

- **Fleeca / Prison Break / Humane Labs / Series A / Pacific Standard:** need 2–4 players, payouts split, poor $/hr. Worth doing once for the **first-time completion bonuses** and the "Criminal Mastermind" challenge; not a money grind.
- **Doomsday Heist:** Act III hard = $2.25M but long and the **finale needs 2 players**. Acts I–II are soloable. Niche; Cayo beats it solo for $/hr.

## What to grind by goal & rank

- **Under rank ~20 / low cash:** Acid Lab First Dose missions, basic contact missions, Cayo as soon as you can buy the Kosatka. Get the nightclub for passive.
- **Mid game:** Cayo loop + nightclub/bunker passive + Agency contracts.
- **Whale / max efficiency:** Cayo + Casino Heist with a partner on alternating cooldowns, full business empire feeding the nightclub, sell only on bonus weeks.
- **Unlocking a specific business:** check this week's **discounts** (MCP `gta-weekly-bonuses`) — buy when the property is on sale.

## General mission tips

- Save **outfits/loadouts** (snacks, armor, suppressed weapons) so you re-enter heists fast.
- Use **invite-only / friend sessions** for setups to avoid griefers; only go public for the high-demand sell bonus.
- For **prep skips/efficiency**, do equipment preps that reduce on-island resistance (disable air defenses/secure servers) so the finale is faster and cleaner.
