# Money Methods — Ranked by $/hr (2025/2026 meta)

Figures are GTA$ and assume relevant upgrades. Verify a value with WebSearch if the player disputes it or a new update just dropped. Where sources disagree, treat these as solid consensus midpoints.

## TL;DR rankings

**Active (effort required), best solo $/hr:**

1. **Agency — Dr. Dre VIP + Payphone/Security loop** — Dre ≈ $1M / 90 min; payphone+security loop ≈ $450k/hr. Best all-around active solo.
2. **Cayo Perico Heist** — ≈ $1.1M–2.3M per ~1 hr run, fully solo. Best per-run solo payout.
3. **Diamond Casino Heist (Diamonds week, 2-player)** — ≈ $3.3M–3.6M/run, needs a partner.
4. **CEO Vehicle Cargo + CEO work (Headhunter/Sightseer)** — ≈ $420k–430k/hr combined.
5. **Auto Shop robbery contracts** — ≈ $300k/hr solo (Union Depository best).
6. **Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid** — ≈ $500k / ~35 min solo, **free** (best for new players).
7. **Salvage Yard robberies** — ≈ $900k–1M but **capped at 3/week**.

**Passive / semi-passive (low effort):**

1. **Acid Lab** — ≈ $120k/hr passive, sell from anywhere. Best solo passive.
2. **Nightclub (techs + safe)** — ≈ $60k–100k/hr accrued while you do other content. Best AFK earner.
3. **Bunker** — ≈ $52k/hr passive.
4. **Cocaine MC** — ≈ $67.5k/hr (Meth/Counterfeit ≈ $43–55k with Money Fronts boosts).

> The **Mansion** (Dec 2025) is not an earner itself but its once-daily 24h **+66% production boost** multiplies whatever passive business you point it at — see `references/properties-catalog.md`.

## Cayo Perico Heist (solo money king)

- **Buy-in:** Kosatka submarine **$2.2M** (one-time). Setup fee **$100k/run**.
- **Primary target (normal / hard +10%):** Tequila $630k/$693k · Necklace $700k/$770k · Bonds $770k/$847k · Pink Diamond $1.3M/$1.43M · **Panther Statue $1.9M/$2.09M** (event-only, rotates in).
- **Secondary loot per stack:** Gold ≈ $330k (best $/bag) > Cocaine ≈ $200k > Artwork ≈ $170k > Weed ≈ $135k > Cash ≈ $80k. Solo carries ~2 bags.
- **Typical full solo run:** ≈ $1.1M–1.7M, up to ~$2.3M+ on Panther + full secondary. **Elite Challenge +$200k** (full loot, <15 min, no failed hacks).
- **Cooldown reality:** the 72-hr timer affects *target rotation*, not your ability to run — you can run back-to-back by re-paying the $100k setup. A ~48-min cooldown applies to re-earning full primary value if you spam. Practical cadence ≈ 1 run/hr with prep.
- **Walkthrough + stealth route:** `references/heists-and-missions.md`.

## Diamond Casino Heist

- **Requires** an Arcade. Setup ~$25k + Avi hacker 10% cut. **Finale needs 2 players** (not soloable).
- **Vault target (normal / hard):** Cash $2.115M/$2.33M · Artwork $2.35M/$2.59M · Gold $2.585M/$2.84M · **Diamonds $3.29M/$3.62M** (only on guaranteed-diamond weeks). Elite +$50k/$100k.
- **2-player split:** ≈ $700k–900k+ each on a good diamonds run. Approaches: Big Con (easiest), Silent & Sneaky (cleanest), Aggressive (loud). Can't repeat the same approach back-to-back.

## Agency — The Contract (Dr. Dre)

- **Property:** Agency $2.01M–$2.83M.
- **Security contracts:** $31k–$70k each (Professional → Specialist+), back-to-back, no cooldown, no sell vehicle.
- **Payphone hits:** $15k base + $30k bonus for the requested kill method = **$45k**, ~10 min each. Loop with security contracts → ~$450k/hr.
- **Dr. Dre VIP contract:** **$1M finale**, repeatable, ~55–90 min solo. First full run of the questline ≈ $1.6M.
- **Safe:** ~$20k/in-game day, caps $250k.

## Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid (free, beginner-friendly)

- **Cost:** free, no property needed. **$500k leader finale** + **$250k first-time bonus** + register/safe cash during the finale. ~30–40 min solo. **60-min cooldown** between runs.
- Best **free** earner for new/mid players; veterans still prefer Cayo for $/hr.

## Auto Shop robbery contracts

- Property ~$1.67M–1.92M. Robbery contracts **$153k–270k** (Union Depository highest) minus 10% staff cut; <30 min → ~$300k/hr solo. Customer cars add small change.

## Salvage Yard robberies

- Property $2.17M–2.9M. **3 of 5 robberies per weekly reset**, ~$260k (Gangbanger) to ~$405k (Podium) each + $50k bonus → ~$900k–1M/week. Tow truck ~$30–40k/car (~$125k/hr). Wall safe ~$24k/in-game day. Hard weekly cap of 3 robberies.

## CEO Vehicle Cargo (Import/Export, top-range method)

- CEO office + Vehicle Warehouse (~$1.5M, 40 cars). Source cars, export **top-range only** for ~$80k +$20k/source ≈ $100k each. ~20-min source cooldown — fill it with CEO work (Headhunter/Sightseer). Combined ≈ $420k–430k/hr. Fully solo.

## CEO Special/Crate Cargo

- CEO office + warehouse (Large = 111 crates ~$2.07M). Full 111-crate sell ≈ $2.2M with bulk bonus; ~$220k/hr solo but selling 111 solo spawns many vehicles — sell in batches. Now mostly a Nightclub feeder.

## Hangar air freight

- Hangar $1.2M–3.25M. Best cargo (Narcotics/Chemicals/Medical) $30k/crate. Full 50-crate = $2.55M, ~$3.83M with high-demand. Solo sources ~12 crates/hr → ~$360k/hr but big sells = multiple aircraft. Shines on 2x weeks. **Higgins Helitours (Money Fronts) adds +10% Hangar cargo value.**

## Bail Office / Bottom Dollar Bounties

- Property $1.65M–2.62M. Standard bounties $35k–40k (alive; 75% penalty if target dies), ~48-min cooldown. **Most Wanted $120k+ once per day.** Agents passive $5k–10k each/in-game day. Lower $/hr than Agency; buy for variety + the daily Most Wanted.

## Classic heists & Doomsday (mostly skip for money)

- **Classic heists** (Fleeca/Prison/Humane/Series A/Pacific Standard): need 2–4 players, payouts split, poor $/hr. Do once for first-time bonuses only.
- **Doomsday:** Act III hard = $2.25M but long and needs 2 players for the finale. Niche; Cayo beats it solo.

## First Dose / Last Dose (do once)

- **First Dose** (6 missions): ~$250k total + unlocks the **free Acid Lab** (essential).
- **Last Dose** (5 missions): **$100k each = $500k** + unlocks. Not repeatable for full value.

## What to grind by goal

- **Fast cash, <30 min:** one Cayo run if ready; else Agency payphone+security loop, or Cluckin' Bell (free), + sell any full business + dailies.
- **Max cash, long session:** Cayo → sell businesses during cooldown → Agency/Salvage → repeat Cayo. Keep nightclub + bunker + acid lab accruing the whole time, mansion-boosted.
- **New / low budget:** First Dose → free Acid Lab → Cluckin' Bell ($500k free) → Nightclub → save for the Kosatka (Cayo). See `references/beginner-roadmap.md`.
