# Optimization Loops — No Wasted Time

The core idea: **passive income should always be accruing while you do active high-$/hr content.** Idle businesses and idle time are lost money. Below are the loops, the math, and the checklists.

## The golden rule of mixing tactics

At the start of every session, in this order:

1. **Activate the Mansion production boost** (once/day, 24 h) on the business you'll sell today — rotate it daily (can't repeat the same business). If no mansion, skip.
2. **Resupply / start production** on every business you own (Bunker, MC, Acid Lab) — buy or steal supplies so they fill while you play.
3. **Assign nightclub technicians** to highest-value goods and top up popularity.
4. **THEN** go do active content (Cayo, Agency contracts, Cluckin' Bell, Casino Heist).
5. **Sell** businesses when full / on their bonus week / before you log off.

You should never be standing around. On a heist cooldown you sell, run contracts, or collect dailies.

## The flagship solo loop (highest sustainable $/hr)

```text
Start session
  └─ Mansion boost → today's target business (rotate daily)
  └─ Resupply Bunker + Acid Lab + MC (buy or steal)
  └─ Set Nightclub techs (Coke/Meth/Cash), top up popularity
Active block (businesses fill in the background)
  └─ Cayo Perico full run (~1 hr) .............. ~$1.1M+
  └─ (Cayo on cooldown?) → Agency payphone+security loop ~$450k/hr
  └─ (or) Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid (free) ....... ~$500k/35 min
  └─ Daily collectibles + safes (~$300k/day, see below)
Sell block (when full or 2x active)
  └─ Sell Acid Lab / Bunker / Nightclub warehouse / Cocaine
  └─ Empty all safes (Nightclub, Agency, Arcade, Auto Shop, Salvage, Bail)
Repeat Cayo when ready
```

Everything in the Active/Sell blocks happens **while** the businesses keep filling. That overlap is the whole game.

## High-demand bonus math (sell location decision)

- Selling a business in a **populated public lobby** grants **+2.5% per rival player, up to +50%** at 20+ players.
- A $1.05M bunker becomes ~$1.575M at full +50% — but you risk griefers blowing up your sell vehicle.
- **Decision:** take the public bonus only if the lobby looks calm, your sell is one fast vehicle, or you have a crew to defend. Otherwise sell **invite-only / solo public** for guaranteed delivery. Combine with a **2x sell week** for the biggest swings.

## When to sell (priority order)

1. **2x/3x bonus this week** for that business → sell now (MCP `gta-weekly-bonuses`).
2. Stock **at/near solo vehicle limit** → sell before a multi-vehicle timed mission.
3. **Nightclub safe near cap (~$250k)** → empty it.
4. About to **log off** → sell everything.

- [ ] **Hidden Caches** ×10 → $100,000

- [ ] **Junk Energy skydives** → up to $50k (all 10 gold one-time = $150k)

- [ ] **Stash House** → free MC resupply (~$75k value) or $30k; weekly: 3 raids → +$100k
- [ ] **Daily Objectives** ×3 → $30,000 (keep the streak)
- [ ] **Lucky Wheel** free spin (podium car chance ~$1M)
- [ ] **Street Dealers** → dump MC/Acid product at a premium
- [ ] Empty all **business safes**
- [ ] **Salvage Yard** robbery if available (3/week)
- [ ] **Bail Office** Most Wanted ($120k+, 1/day)

## Weekly checklist (resets Thursday 07:00 UTC)

- [ ] Check the new **weekly event**: which activities are 2x/3x, the **podium vehicle**, **discounts**, bonus GTA$/RP (MCP `gta-weekly-bonuses`).
- [ ] Plan big **sells** around whichever business is bonused this week.
- [ ] If a **heist/Cayo target** is bonused, prioritize it.
- [ ] **LS Car Meet prize ride** (free ~$1M car for the weekly challenge).
- [ ] Reset your **Salvage Yard** 3 robberies and **Daily Objective** streak goals.

## Time-boxing by session length

- **15–20 min:** one Cayo run (if ready) OR sell one full business + collect dailies/safes.
- **30–45 min:** Cayo → repeat or Agency loop → dailies.
- **1–2 hr:** full loop — boost, resupply, Cayo, sells, contracts, dailies, repeat Cayo.
- **AFK:** stand on the nightclub dance floor (safe fills) or leave production running; never log off with full sellable stock unsold.

## Common time-wasters to avoid

- Running **Document Forgery / base Weed** for active sells (low ROI) — feed them to the nightclub or skip.
- Selling small amounts repeatedly (fixed travel time per sell — batch larger).
- **Grief wars** in public instead of earning — go invite-only/solo to sell (see `references/combat-and-survival.md`).
- Buying supplies when you have time to **steal** them (only if your time isn't better spent on Cayo).
- Sitting through a Cayo cooldown doing nothing — that's prime time for contracts, sells, and dailies.
- Wasting the **Mansion boost** on the Nightclub warehouse (not eligible) or forgetting to rotate it daily.
