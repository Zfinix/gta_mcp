# Property Catalog & Buy Priority (incl. the Mansion)

Every income-relevant property, what it unlocks, and the order to buy. Costs are approximate base prices (2025/2026); upgrades add more. Pair with `references/businesses.md` for mechanics.

## Buy priority (new/returning player)

1. **First Dose missions → free Acid Lab** (install $750k, free w/ GTA+). Cheapest strong solo income.
2. **Nightclub** ($1.08M–1.7M) — passive backbone; buy near your other businesses for fast promos.
3. **MC Clubhouse + Cocaine Lockup** (~$200k + ~$1.9M) — best MC business + nightclub feeder.
4. **CEO Office** (Securoserv, $1M+) — unlocks VIP work, Vehicle/Special Cargo.
5. **Kosatka submarine** ($2.2M) — unlocks **Cayo Perico**, the income engine. Prioritize this.
6. **Agency** ($2.01M+) — The Contract: Dr. Dre ($1M) + security/payphone loop.
7. **Bunker** ($1.165M+) — big passive sells + nightclub feeder.
8. **Arcade** ($1.235M+) — unlocks **Diamond Casino Heist** (the $5k/day is incidental).
9. **Salvage Yard** ($2.17M+) — ~$1M/week from 3 robberies.
10. **Auto Shop** ($1.67M+) — solo robbery contracts (~$300k/hr).
11. **Money Fronts** (Car Wash $1M / Smoke $850k) — only if you grind Counterfeit/Weed (+35% each).
12. **Bail Office** ($1.65M+) — variety + daily Most Wanted ($120k).
13. **(Endgame) Mansion** ($11.5M+) — luxury + the daily +66% production boost.

## The Mansion — deep dive

**What it is:** the **Prix Luxury Real Estate** mansions from the **"A Safehouse in the Hills" update (Title Update 1.72, Dec 10 2025)** — the newest and most expensive property class in the game. Three options, you can own all three:

| Mansion | Base price | Location |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| The Tongva Estate | $11,500,000 | Tongva Hills (Devin Weston's GTA V estate) |
| The Vinewood Residence | $12,200,000 | East Vinewood Hills |
| Richman Villa | $12,800,000 | Richman |

Fully upgraded with all optional rooms is **>$17.5M**. No rank/business prerequisite to buy.

**It makes NO passive income of its own.** Its money value is three things:

1. **AI Assistant "Business Production Boost"** — once per day, **24-hour, ~66% production-speed boost (effectively ~3x output)** on ONE chosen business. Eligible: Acid Lab, Bunker, Cocaine, Meth, Weed, Counterfeit, Document Forgery. **NOT the Nightclub warehouse.** You **must rotate** — can't boost the same business two days in a row. Resets at daily reset. Approx boosted $/hr: Cocaine ~$315k, Counterfeit ~$268k, Bunker ~$262k, Acid Lab ~$263k, Meth ~$223–446k (doubles on bonus weeks). Activate via the iFruit AI Assistant contact, mansion wall tablets, or Interaction Menu. **Stack with the Acid Lab's own Chemical Boost.**
2. **Master Control Terminal** (in the Office, comes with the base mansion) — manage/launch all your businesses remotely instead of driving to each one.
3. **One-time mission payouts:** the **KnoWay Out** 5-mission arc (Avi Schwartzman; doesn't need a mansion; finale ≈ $525k) and the **mansion-exclusive Michael De Santa mission "Home Sweet Home"** (requires owning a mansion + finishing KnoWay Out; **first completion $500k**, replays ~$30–40k).

**Optional upgrades** (convenience/cosmetic, none increase the boost): Car Podium $650k, Armory $720k, Vehicle Workshop $880k, Arcade $950k, Private Security $1.75M (raid alerts), interior décor up to ~$612k. AI personas (Angel/Haviland/OG) are purely cosmetic.

**Verdict & routine:** buy the mansion **only after** you already own the businesses it boosts — it amplifies income, it doesn't bootstrap it. Daily: activate the boost on the business you'll actively sell that day (Acid Lab is the solo pick), rotating each day, redirecting to whatever's on the weekly 2x. Note: the launch **New Listings $1M bonus + up to $2M discount** and the **KnoWay Out $400k weekly challenge** were limited-time and have ended.
